## What NOT to do

- Do not approach mine/quarry **employers** directly as a data source or deployment partner —
  the PRD's §5 deployment-channel risk section is explicit that employer-adjacent deployment
  carries materially higher legal and ethical risk for informal-sector workers with no
  statutory bargaining power. Worker-side NGOs and welfare boards are the correct channel.
- Do not promise any organization a working silicosis detector before Stage 2 data exists.
  Every outreach message above frames this as "help us find out if a signal exists," not
  "we have a tool that detects silicosis" — keep it that way in any follow-up too.
